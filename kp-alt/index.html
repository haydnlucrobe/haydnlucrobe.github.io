The Addams Family Musical

Derby Guildhall Theatre - 9th-11th July, 2016

The weird and wonderful Addams come to life in an original story, and it's every father's nightmare... Wednesday Addams, the ultimate princess of darkness, has grown up and fallen in love with a sweet, smart young man from a respectable family. A man her parents have never met. And if that weren't upsetting enough, she confides in her father and begs him not to tell her mother.
Now, Gomez Addams must do something he's never done before - keep a secret from his beloved wife, Morticia. Everything will change for the whole family on the fateful night they host a dinner for Wednesday's normal boyfriend and his parents.
Come meet the family. We'll leave the lights off for you.
